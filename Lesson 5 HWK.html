Introduction:

Shakespeare is one of the most famous playwrights in the English Language. He has written numerous plays like Hamlet and Macbeth and has also written some of the most recognisable speeches in history, like To Be or Not to Be. Below is a picture of Shakespeare.


Shakepeare also has written a diverse range of plays, ranging from tragedies to comedies and romantic plays. Early Life
